Milestone Eight

KANAZAWA

Watercolor OpenStreetMap Burn This Map

This map of Kanazawa, Ishikawa Prefecture, Japan, is an exercise in map abstraction and the limitations of open-source layers. While Bing Maps translates and/or romanizes labels from the local language into English/roman characters, its tiles are not open-source. Stamen's terrain tiles offer limited romanization, while OpenStreetMap maintains local languages and characters. The watercolor layer builds on this, as features go unlabeled entirely and the change in representations across scales is emphasized.

Thanks for reading. 2017.
