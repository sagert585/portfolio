Milestone Nine

Health Data in New Mexico

Small health areas – seen here in black lines with dark shading – are the level at which the New Mexico Department of Health's IBIS system aggregates the health outcome data it releases to the public. This aggregation is done to protect the confidentiality of patients. Maroon stars represent a selection of health facilities across the state - hospitals, urgent care facilities, and nursing homes. Finally, facilities offering mammograms are shown by red squares (UNM facilities), purple squares (Lovelace facilities), green triangles (X-ray Associates), yellow triangles (High Resolution locations), and a blue circle (Albuquerque Health Partners).
