Watercolor | Learn Japanese

This map of Kanazawa, Ishikawa Prefecture, Japan, is an exercise in map abstraction and the limitations of open-source layers. While Bing Maps translates and/or romanizes labels from the local language into English/roman characters, its tiles are not open-source. Stamen's terrain tiles offer limited romanization, while OpenStreetMap maintains local languages and characters.
